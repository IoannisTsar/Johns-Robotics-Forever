My Story - John's Robotics

My Story

My robotics journey is fascinating, from simple stem kits to an upcoming robot built from scratch. So lets dive deeper into my journey so far.

Pre-after-school-club

When i was younger i had some STEM/STEAM kits, i liked them but what i think is the biggest milestone for me and my robotics skills is the preperation before the robotics after-school-club that my maths teacher is in charge.

The after-school-club preperation

When i was preparing for the robotics club i learned to use Arduino (i knew that Arduino existed but it was a far away world for me that i would never reach), i also learnt about ESP32/ESP8266 and raspberry pi.

In the after-school-club

When i got into the club i learned more about the above topics and made some projects. We got an rpi pico W wich we will use to build a robot car. And we will also make a stereoscopic vision car using raspberry pi 5.
